About This Website

Purpose of creation: This website was created to help any car buyers (though having a license and good credit score would be a bonus) regardless of age, gender, education, or socio-economic status find cars based on their preferences. Cars ranging from the years 2006-2017 are listed on this site. If you would like to submit feedback, please do so on the Contact Us webpage or email me directly using the email at the bottom of this webpage. Thank you and enjoy this website.

Home page: The Home page consists of our top car picks. The car pictures range from Years 2016-2017. Each picture contains a link to the corresponding year page (example: if a car is from the year 2008, the picture on the home page will link to a section on the Years 2006-2009 page.) The corresponding year page will have more details on the car.

Factors to consider: This webpage contains key factors to review before purchasing a car. Some key factors listed are mileage, MPG, price, and fuel type. Each key factor is explained in more detail. In addition, a sample table is provided in order to aid you when purchasing a car.
